ABOUT FESTIVALS

Festivals.com is an online free content project helping to create a world in which everyone can freely share in the sum of all knowledge concerning festivals. It is assigned by the Aptech management and based on a cumulative assessment in order to monitor the progress rate of her students. The name "festivals.com" was used in order to hint the website visitor(s) a vague insight on what the site is all about.
